Mutekiya

無敵家

In the middle of Ikebukuro, Mutekiya is a unmissable ramen restaurant if your looking for culinary discovery.
"Mutekiya" literaly means "undefeatable" in japanese, and they intend to keep their reputation.
Large scale of choices and tastes in approach!
